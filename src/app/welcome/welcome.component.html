Welcome to Angular Skeleton

This is a sample Angular application, using Angular Material for visual design. This is the beginning of a baseline app to be used for future projects, with a layout I've used for multiple projects.

A welcome page gives users a description of the app and prompts them to log in, though the welcome page may be dropped in favor of sending users directly to login.

Go ahead and log in. The sample app is not connected to a back end service, so does not actually perform any login verification. Any email and password will do.
